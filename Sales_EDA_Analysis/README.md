# 📊 Sales Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project demonstrates a complete Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) workflow using the Global Superstore dataset.

The objective is to understand the dataset, identify important business patterns, visualize trends, and extract meaningful insights that can support data-driven decision making.

The project covers statistical analysis, data visualization, trend analysis, customer segmentation, regional performance, profitability analysis, and business recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Understand the structure of the dataset
- Perform statistical analysis
- Check missing values and duplicate records
- Analyze customer segments
- Analyze regional sales performance
- Study shipping preferences
- Explore sales and profit relationships
- Examine the impact of discounts on profit
- Analyze monthly and yearly sales trends
- Identify top-performing states and product sub-categories
- Generate actionable business insights

## 📊 Key Insights

- Sales increase significantly during the final quarter of the year.
- November records the highest monthly sales.
- Sales show consistent growth after 2015, with 2017 achieving the highest annual sales.
- California generates the highest overall sales.
- The Consumer segment contributes the largest number of customer orders.
- Office Supplies is the most frequently ordered product category.
- Standard Class is the preferred shipping method.
- Copiers, Phones, and Accessories generate the highest profits.
- Larger discounts are associated with lower profitability.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory before peak sales months.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing states.
- Strengthen customer retention within the Consumer segment.
- Promote highly profitable product categories.
- Optimize discount strategies to improve profit margins.
- Continue monitoring yearly sales growth for long-term planning.
